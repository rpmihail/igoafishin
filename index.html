About Your Guide

I’m Hayden Horne, a second generation charter captain with deep roots in commercial and offshore fishing. I specialize in nearshore and offshore charter trips, helping families and anglers of all experience levels enjoy time on the water and the thrill of a great catch. My father, Jeff Horne, serves as co captain on our offshore charters and brings over 40 years of commercial and charter fishing experience, both inshore and offshore. Together, we run a family owned charter fishing business built on faith, hard work, and a genuine love for what we do. Come fish with us and experience why this isn’t just our job. It’s our calling.
